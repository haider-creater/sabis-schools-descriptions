About SABIS®

SABIS® is a global education network that operates schools across five continents. With a proven track record of success spanning over 130 years, SABIS® provides a high-quality education to thousands of students worldwide. The SABIS® Educational System is a comprehensive, rigorous, and internationally-oriented curriculum designed to prepare students for success in college and beyond.
